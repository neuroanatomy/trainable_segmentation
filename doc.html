MicroDraw URL. Provide the link to your MicroDraw project and the slice where you do the segmentations. You can segment in one slice a few regions that you consider helpful for the classifier to train on your data, and you can iteratively modify your segmentation.

Token. Provide your access token for the MicroDraw project. Log into MicroDraw and go to https://microdraw.pasteur.fr/token, the project where you have the segmenttations, eventually the token. Iterate until you are happy. Then send the output segmentation back t MicroDraw.

Send to MicroDraw. Sends the resulting segmentation from the random forest classifier back to your MicroDraw project.

MicroDraw Trainable Segmentation interface & workflow. Provide the link to your MicroDraw project with the segmentations and your access token. Edit your segmentations and iterate until you are happy. Then send the output segmentation back to MicroDraw.
